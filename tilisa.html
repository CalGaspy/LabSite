Current Roles:

I’m developing an indoor UAV system that enables reliable autonomous flight in GPS-denied environments by using onboard sensing for localization, obstacle detection, and navigation. The system relies on sensor based perception to map and understand its surroundings in real time, allowing it to plan safe paths and maintain stable flight indoors where GPS is unavailable.

Research interests:

  • Designing UAVs
  • Efficient onboard computation of image and acoustic data on UAVs
  • Autonomous indoor UAV/Robot navigation
  • Flight Controller development
  • Machine Learning for Microcontrollers

Education:
