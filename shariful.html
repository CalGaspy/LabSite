About Shariful!

Shariful is an experienced embedded software and firmware developer with a strong passion for building reliable, high-performance systems. He has hands-on experience designing and developing custom quadcopter platforms and integrating specialized imaging systems for landscape scanning and mapping. His work combines low-level firmware development with full system integration to deliver robust, field-ready aerial solutions.

Current Roles:

Shariful is currently driving research on custom quadcopter development, pushing forward new platform designs, embedded firmware, and integrated sensing capabilities for reliable, field-ready aerial solutions.

Research interests:

  • Advanced embedded systems
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Custom quadcopter development
